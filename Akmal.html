ðŸ”¶ Smart Emergency Response Control Centre (SERCC)

The Smart Emergency Response Control Centre is the heart of the emergency ecosystem. It acts as a unified digital platform that coordinates ambulance services, fire departments, and police units. The system continuously monitors the city and allocates the nearest available vehicle for every emergency call.

  • Real-time vehicle tracking using GPS.
  • Predicts response time using live traffic conditions.
  • Automated rerouting if roads are blocked or under construction.
  • Single dashboard to monitor availability of all emergency units.
  • Historical data helps improve emergency planning and reduce delays.

Impact: Reduces response time, improves transparency, and increases efficiency during peak emergency hours.

ðŸ”¶ Disaster Evacuation & Safe Shelter Planning

Cities must be prepared for floods, fires, and industrial accidents. This system uses simulations and graph-based algorithms to identify safe evacuation routes and quickly guide people to shelter zones.

  • Uses BFS/DFS to find reachable shelters.
  • Identifies bottlenecks and choke points on evacuation routes.
  • Predicts disaster outcomes based on historical data.
  • Automated alerting system for vulnerable zones.

Impact: Saves lives during disasters and strengthens city resilience.

ðŸ”¶ Crime Hotspot Detection & Predictive Policing

By analyzing emergency call data, the system can identify areas with frequent accidents, crimes, or safety issues. Using analytics and graph traversal techniques, it recommends optimized patrol routes for police units.

  • Generates heatmaps for high-risk areas.
  • Detects time-based crime patterns.
  • Suggests shortest patrol routes using graph algorithms.
  • Improves street safety with data-backed planning.

ðŸ”¶ Integrated Fire Hazard Monitoring & Rapid Response

Fires spread rapidly and require coordinated response. This subsystem integrates sensors, hydrant locations, and routing engines to ensure the fastest dispatch of fire trucks.

  • Fire sensors send instant alerts to control centre.
  • Maps show nearest hydrants and fire stations.
  • Considers road width & slope for fire truck routing.
  • Predicts fire spread direction using wind and weather data.

Impact: Strengthens fire safety, reduces destruction, and speeds up firefighting response.
