ðŸ”¶ Smart Emergency Response Control Centre (SERCC)

The Smart Emergency Response Control Centre acts as the central command hub for all emergency services. It provides a unified digital platform for monitoring, dispatching, and coordinating ambulances, fire engines, and police units across the city.

  • Live tracking of all emergency vehicles.
  • Automatic dispatch based on proximity and availability.
  • Predicts response time using real-time traffic data.
  • Dynamic rerouting during roadblocks or congestion.
  • Enhances coordination during peak times and major incidents.

Impact: Faster responses, reduced delays, and improved emergency handling efficiency.

ðŸ”¶ Intelligent Ambulance Routing Network

This subsystem uses weighted graph algorithms to determine the fastest possible path for ambulances. By considering traffic density, road width, signals, and accident-prone spots, it creates a truly smart routing system.

Feature Description
Real-time routing Continuously updates the fastest path
Smart traffic coordination Turns signals green on ambulance path
Congestion prediction Uses stored patterns to avoid high-traffic zones
Fail-safe routing Switches routes instantly when the primary path fails

Impact: Reduces ambulance delay by up to 60%, saving countless lives.

ðŸ”¶ Crime Hotspot Detection & Predictive Policing

By analyzing call logs and emergency data, the system can identify locations with frequent crimes, accidents, or late-night disturbances. The insights help authorities plan effective patrol routes.

  • Generates visual heatmaps of risky zones.
  • Detects time-based crime trends (night thefts, weekend accidents).
  • Suggests shortest, most efficient patrol routes.
  • Supports city planning for safer public spaces.

ðŸ”¶ Integrated Fire Hazard Monitoring & Rapid Response

Fires can escalate rapidly. This subsystem combines sensor networks, weather data, and routing algorithms to ensure the fastest arrival of fire engines and prevent secondary fires.

  • Automatic alerts from fire sensors installed across the city.
  • Maps show nearest fire stations and hydrants.
  • Considers road width & slope to choose best truck route.
  • Predicts fire spread direction using wind patterns.

Impact: Saves infrastructure, prevents fire spread, and reduces casualties.
