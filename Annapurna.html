UIONC – Urban Information & Operations Nerve Center

About the Project

The Urban Information & Operations Nerve Center (UIONC) is Navapura's central city coordination hub, enabling unified governance across traffic, utilities, agriculture, emergency response, waste management, public transport, energy, disaster-handling, security, and more. This system uses an extensive range of Data Structures and Algorithms, far beyond typical city-planning projects, making it the most technically comprehensive and scalable model.
