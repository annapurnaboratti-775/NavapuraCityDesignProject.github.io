Design & Analysis of Algorithms

A structured journey into computational thinking, efficiency analysis, and problem-solving through powerful algorithms and elegant data structures.

Introduction

The Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) course builds the foundations of efficient computing by teaching structured approaches for problem solving, performance analysis, and algorithm design patterns such as greedy strategies, dynamic programming, divide-and-conquer, and graph algorithms.
