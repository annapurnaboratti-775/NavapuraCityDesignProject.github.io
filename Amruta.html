System Overview

The Smart City Service Automation & Routing System provides a unified platform for managing essential city services such as healthcare, waste collection, utilities, parking, and industrial production. Each module works independently while sharing data through a centralized architecture, ensuring seamless coordination.

By integrating graph-based models, efficient data structures, and priority-driven logic, the system optimizes routing, resource distribution, and real-time decision-making.
